Diversity Statement

Growing up in Pittsburgh PA, my journey has been a mosaic of cultures, traditions, and perspectives. From a young age, I learned the value of listening to stories that echoed both the struggles and triumphs of individuals from various walks of life. These diverse narratives painted a vibrant picture of our interconnected world, fostering in me a deep appreciation for the strength that emerges when people of different backgrounds come together with open hearts and minds. I am committed to fostering an inclusive society where everyone is seen, heard, and valued. I believe that true togetherness requires not just the acknowledgment of our differences but the celebration of them. In every interaction, I strive to create spaces where individuals feel empowered to express their authentic selves, contributing their unique insights to the collective wisdom that propels us forward.
